Biography

Aaron Di Silvestro is a data engineer, reporting analyst, machine learning enthusiast, software developer, and coffee drinker. He enjoys the squishy middle between actual data and business decisions and thinks that we could all be a little more optimal.

His life’s work is to leave the world a better place than when he started and so he’s happy to be working at Nike’s Sustainable Business and Innovation with the Advanced Analyitics team.

He doubts very much that email inboxes were a good idea but is happy to stop talking about it because he’s socially aware enough to recognize bemused eye rolls.

What?

Avalanche forecast will, with realistic terrain, weather, and snowpack modeling, provide an avalanche forecasting experience. The final game will balance education and entertainment in a way that targets enthusiastic backcountry recreators.

Status

Current work is focused on procedural terrain generation and display. The terrain generation engine must consistently produce known avalanche terrain on arbitrary aspects. Display is targetting a terminal UI for fun and speed.
